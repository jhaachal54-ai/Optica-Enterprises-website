About Optica Enterprises

A trusted partner in diagnostic medicine — supplying precision instruments, providing expert guidance, and supporting healthcare across the region.

Who We Are

Built Around a Simple Purpose

Optica Enterprises was founded with a clear mission: to make high-quality, clinically proven diagnostic equipment accessible to healthcare providers across the region. We believe that accurate diagnostics are the foundation of good medicine, and that every clinic and laboratory — regardless of size — deserves access to world-class instruments.

As an authorised distributor for globally recognised brands including Agappe Diagnostics, Getein Biotech, OPTI Medical Systems, EXIAS Medical, and Wondfo, we bring a comprehensive portfolio covering hematology, clinical chemistry, immunoassay, coagulation, electrolyte analysis, and critical care diagnostics.

Beyond sales, we provide full after-sales support — from installation and operator training to periodic calibration and on-site servicing — because we know that the right instrument is only as good as the support behind it.

Our Story

How We Got Here

🌱

Founded with a Vision

Optica Enterprises was established with a focused vision: to bridge the gap between world-class diagnostic technology and healthcare providers across the region who lacked access to high-quality instruments at fair prices.

🤝

First Brand Partnerships

We secured our first distribution agreements with leading manufacturers, beginning with Agappe Diagnostics — a partnership that gave us a comprehensive portfolio spanning hematology, chemistry, and immunoassay right from the start.

📈

Growing Our Portfolio

Expanding relationships with Getein Biotech, OPTI Medical Systems, EXIAS Medical, and Wondfo allowed us to offer a complete diagnostic solution — from routine blood counts to critical care blood gas analysis.

🔧

Building After-Sales Excellence

Recognising that equipment support is as important as the equipment itself, we invested in building a trained service team capable of providing installation, calibration, and maintenance across our entire product range.

🚀

Serving 100+ Clients Today

Today, Optica Enterprises serves over 100 clinics, hospitals, and diagnostic laboratories. We remain committed to the same values that drove our founding — quality, reliability, and genuine care for our clients.
