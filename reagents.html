Why Use Genuine Reagents?

Approved reagents are validated against each instrument's calibration matrix. Substitutes introduce variability that affects clinical decisions.

🎯

Guaranteed Accuracy

Manufacturer reagents are lot-validated to the instrument's calibration. Third-party alternatives introduce bias that can silently compromise result reliability.

🛡️

Warranty Protection

All warranties require the use of approved consumables. Using non-genuine supplies may void coverage — we supply everything needed to keep you compliant.

📦

Ready Stock & Supply

We maintain buffer stock of fast-moving reagents so your laboratory never faces supply gaps. Contact us to set up a regular supply arrangement.
