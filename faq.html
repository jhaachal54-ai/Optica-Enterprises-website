General

Optica Enterprises is a specialised distributor of medical diagnostic instruments. We supply clinics, hospitals, and diagnostic laboratories with precision analysers for hematology, clinical chemistry, immunoassay, coagulation, electrolyte testing, and critical care. We also provide full after-sales support including installation, training, calibration, and repair.
Our team operates across the region and provides equipment supply and service support to clients in and around our service area. For specific location details or to confirm whether we cover your area, please contact us directly via phone, WhatsApp, or our contact form.
You can reach us by phone at +91 76782 73175 or +91 99100 63557, via WhatsApp using the floating button on this website, or by filling in our Contact Form. We aim to respond to all enquiries within a few hours during business days.
Yes. Optica Enterprises holds authorised distributor status for all the brands in our portfolio. This means the instruments we supply are genuine products with full manufacturer support and warranties. We can provide distributor authorisation letters on request.

Products

We carry instruments from Agappe Diagnostics (India), Getein Biotech (China), OPTI Medical Systems (USA), EXIAS Medical, and Wondfo (China). Our product range covers hematology analysers, clinical chemistry analysers (semi-automated and fully automated), immunoassay systems, coagulation analysers, HbA1c analysers, specific protein analysers, electrolyte analysers, and critical care blood gas analysers.
Yes. All instruments we distribute are sourced from manufacturers whose products carry appropriate international certifications such as CE marking, ISO 13485 quality management certification, and CDSCO registration where applicable. Certification documentation is available on request for any product.

Extended warranty and annual maintenance contracts (AMC) are also available through us. Please contact our team for warranty terms specific to a product you are interested in.

Service & Support

Yes — installation and operator training are included with every instrument we supply. Our service engineer handles the physical installation, connects the instrument to your LIS if required, and conducts hands-on training for all staff who will operate the system. Additional refresher training can be arranged at any time.
We aim to respond to all breakdown reports on the same business day. For urgent situations, our team first attempts remote troubleshooting to get you back up and running as quickly as possible. If a site visit is required, we coordinate the earliest available appointment and aim to attend within 24–48 hours.
Yes. Annual maintenance contracts are available for all instruments we supply. An AMC typically covers a set number of scheduled preventive maintenance visits per year, priority response for breakdown calls, and discounted or included consumable parts. Contact us for AMC pricing tailored to your instrument and usage.
In some cases, yes. If you have an instrument from one of the brands we represent that was not originally purchased through us, contact our team to discuss service options. We will advise on availability based on the specific model and condition.
