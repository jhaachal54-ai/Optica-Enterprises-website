After-Sales Service & Support

Our commitment doesn't end at the sale. We provide comprehensive support to keep your diagnostic instruments performing at their best — every day.

What We Offer

Complete Service Coverage

From the moment your instrument is delivered to years of reliable daily use — we're with you at every step.

📦

Installation & Commissioning

Our trained engineers handle the complete installation of every instrument we supply — from physical setup and electrical connections to software configuration. We commission the analyser, verify performance against manufacturer specifications, and confirm readiness for clinical use before handing over to your team.

🎓

Operator Training

Every installation includes hands-on training for your laboratory staff. We cover daily operation, sample preparation, quality control procedures, troubleshooting common alerts, and routine maintenance tasks. Training is tailored to the experience level of your team and can be refreshed at any time on request.

🔩

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled preventive maintenance visits keep your instruments operating at peak performance and extend their service life. Our engineers inspect critical components, replace consumable parts, clean internal pathways, verify calibration, and provide a detailed service report after every visit.

🛠️

Breakdown & Repair Service

When something goes wrong, we act fast. Our service team responds promptly to breakdown calls, carrying commonly required spare parts to minimise downtime. For complex repairs, we coordinate with the manufacturer to source parts quickly and restore your instrument to full function as soon as possible.

📐

Calibration & Verification

Accurate results depend on accurate calibration. We perform calibration and performance verification for all instruments in our portfolio, using certified reference materials and manufacturer-approved protocols. Calibration certificates are provided for instruments requiring documentation for accreditation or regulatory compliance.

📞

Remote Technical Support

Many issues can be resolved without a site visit. Our technical support team is available by phone and messaging to guide your staff through troubleshooting steps, assist with software queries, interpret error codes, and advise on QC failure situations — minimising disruption to your workflow.

How It Works

Our Service Process

A simple, structured process so you always know what to expect.

1

Contact Us

Call, WhatsApp, or fill our contact form to report an issue or schedule a service visit. We respond within a few hours.

2

Initial Assessment

Our engineer performs a remote diagnosis to understand the issue and determine whether a site visit or remote resolution is appropriate.

3

Service Visit or Remote Fix

We attend site with the necessary tools and parts, or guide your team through the fix remotely. The instrument is tested thoroughly before the job is closed.

4

Follow-Up & Documentation

We confirm instrument performance, provide a service report, and check in after a few days to ensure everything is running smoothly.
