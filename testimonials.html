Case Study 01

Upgrading a 3-Lab Chain to Fully Automated Chemistry

📍 Delhi NCR 🏥 Multi-site diagnostic chain 🔬 Mispa CXL Pro Plus × 3

Challenge: A growing diagnostic chain with three locations was running semi-automated chemistry analyzers at all sites. Staff turnover and inconsistent technique were causing inter-site result discrepancies, and throughput could not keep up with growing patient volumes.

Solution: Optica Enterprises recommended the Mispa CXL Pro Plus (240 tests/hr, 60-reagent positions, bidirectional LIS) for all three sites. A unified reagent supply arrangement and standardised QC protocol were implemented across all locations.

Outcome: Inter-site coefficient of variation for key analytes fell below 3%. Throughput increased by 280%. Reagent cost per test reduced by 22% compared to the previous open-system setup. All three sites now share patient result data via a common LIS without calibration discrepancies.

Case Study 02

Enabling Point-of-Care Cardiac Testing in an Emergency Department

📍 Gurugram, Haryana 🏥 100-bed private hospital 🔬 Getein 1100 + OPTI CCA TS 2

Challenge: The emergency department was sending cardiac marker and blood gas samples to the central lab, resulting in a 60–90 minute turnaround that delayed triage decisions for chest pain and suspected MI patients.

Solution: Optica installed the Getein 1100 (Troponin, NT-proBNP, D-Dimer in 3–15 min) and the OPTI CCA TS 2 (blood gas in under 2 min) in the ED. Staff were trained to operate both instruments with minimal prep during the handover shift.

Outcome: Average cardiac marker result time dropped from 72 minutes to 12 minutes. Blood gas results are now available before the physician has completed the initial patient assessment. The ED team reports improved confidence in triage categorisation for cardiac and respiratory patients.
